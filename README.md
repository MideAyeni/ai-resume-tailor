# 📄 Resume Tailor

**Resume Tailor** is an AI-powered web application designed to help job seekers customize their resumes for specific roles. By analyzing a target job description alongside your current resume, it generates tailored rewrites to help you stand out to recruiters and hiring managers.

## ✨ How to Use

Using Resume Tailor is quick and easy:

1. **Paste the Job Description:** Copy the full text of the job posting you want to apply for and paste it into the "Job Description" box on the left.
2. **Upload Your Resume:** Click the upload box on the right to attach your current resume. *(Note: Please ensure your resume is a text-based PDF).*
3. **Tailor It:** Click the **"Tailor my resume"** button at the bottom.
4. **Review Your Results:** The AI will process the information and provide customized resume rewrites perfectly aligned with the job requirements.
